About

Who we are

  • 2019-2021

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We met in our country and we started to work together on our firsts challenging projects. Finding the good balance betwween life, university, work and fun was struggling. That was the time we realized we needed to do something different.

  • By June 2021

    An Agency will Born

    With our firts works going in the right direction, we want to create a company. This will help us in finding new beautiful people that shares our feelings and desires. We will start to build our world, brick after brick, person after person.

  • By December 2021

    International

    We want to become international. With our name spreading over the internet, we want to find our firsts abroad collaborators and friends.

  • Future

    Expansion

    We want to find people with our motivation, with our passion and with our dreams. We want to create a family, we want to be alive. We want work to live, not live to work!
